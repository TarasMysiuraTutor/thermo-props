## Thermophysical properties of air

Air is a gaseous mixture that can be approximated as an ideal gas
under normal engineering conditions. Its thermophysical properties
are primarily governed by temperature and pressure.

This page provides basic engineering calculations of air properties
for educational and practical use. The adopted model emphasizes
simplicity, numerical stability, and clearly stated physical assumptions.

### Typical applications
- Heat exchangers  
- Ventilation and air-conditioning systems  
- Gas flow and aerodynamics  
- Preliminary engineering estimates  

### Limitations
Real-gas effects at elevated pressures or near condensation conditions
are not considered.
